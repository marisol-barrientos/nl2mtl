ID 1: When the vehicle's speed exceeds 100 kilometers per hour, a distance of 100 meters or more must be maintained from the vehicle ahead in the same lane
  • Proposition ID vehicle_speed_exceeds_100: Vehicle's speed exceeds 100 kilometers per hour
    • Granularity: seconds
  • Proposition ID distance_from_front_vehicle_is_100_or_more: Distance from the vehicle in front is 100 meters or more
    • Granularity: seconds
  • MTL Expression: G(vehicle_speed_exceeds_100 ⇒ X(distance_from_front_vehicle_is_100_or_more))
ID 2: When the vehicle's speed is below 100 kilometers per hour, the distance from the vehicle in front may be narrowed, but not less than 50 meters
  • Proposition ID vehicle_speed_below_100: Vehicle's speed is below 100 kilometers per hour
    • Granularity: seconds
  • Proposition ID distance_from_front_vehicle_is_50_or_more: Distance from the vehicle in front is 50 meters or more
    • Granularity: seconds
  • MTL Expression: G(vehicle_speed_below_100 ⇒ X(distance_from_front_vehicle_is_50_or_more))

Dependency Sequence

From ID 1 to ID 2: The dependency relation represents a mutual exclusion. Only one of the two scenarios can happen at any given point

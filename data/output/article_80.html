Article 80

MTL Expressions

ID MTL1: Motor vehicle speed exceeds 100 kilometers per hour
  • Proposition ID p1: Speed of the vehicle exceeds 100 kmph
    • Assumption: The speedometer of the vehicle is functioning properly
    • Ambiguity: The temporal granularity could differ based on the speed of the vehicle
    • Granularity: seconds
  • MTL Expression: G[p1]
ID MTL2: Vehicle maintains a distance of 100 meters or more from the vehicle in front in the same lane when speed exceeds 100 kilometers per hour
  • Proposition ID p2: Vehicle maintains a distance of 100 meters or more from the vehicle in front in the same vehicle lane
    • Assumption: The distance indicator of the vehicle is functioning properly
    • Ambiguity: The temporal granularity of maintaining the distance is not mentioned
    • Granularity: seconds
  • MTL Expression: G[(speed > 100) -> (distance >= 100)]
ID MTL3: When the vehicle speed is lower than 100 kmph, the distance from the vehicle in front may be narrowed, but not less than 50 meters
  • Proposition ID p3: Vehicle maintains a distance of 50 meters or more, but less than 100 meters, from the vehicle in front in the same vehicle lane when speed is less than 100 kilometers per hour
    • Assumption: The distance and speed indicators of the vehicle are functioning properly
    • Ambiguity: The temporal granularity of maintaining the distance is not mentioned
    • Granularity: seconds
  • MTL Expression: G[(speed < 100) -> (50 <= distance < 100)]

Dependency Sequence

From ID MTL1 to ID MTL2: Speed exceeding 100 kilometers per hour, leads to maintaining a specific distance.
From ID MTL2 to ID MTL3: When speed reduces below 100 kilometers per hour, the distance can be narrowed, but not less than 50 meters.

Non Formalized Parts

ID NF1: narrowed appropriately (Reason: This phrase is subjective and depends on the judgement of the driver. Hence, it cannot be formalized.)

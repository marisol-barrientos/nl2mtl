Article 78

MTL Expressions

ID MTL_1: Speeds for different vehicle lanes on an expressway are indicated.
  • Proposition ID P1: Speeds for different vehicle lanes on an expressway are indicated
    • Granularity: continuous
  • MTL Expression: G(speeds_indicated)
ID MTL_2: The maximum speed does not exceed 120 kilometers per hour and the minimum speed is not lower than 60 kilometers per hour.
  • Proposition ID P2: The maximum speed does not exceed 120 kilometers per hour
    • Granularity: continuous
  • Proposition ID P3: The minimum speed is not lower than 60 kilometers per hour
    • Granularity: continuous
  • MTL Expression: G(((max_speed ≤ 120) ∧ (min_speed ≥ 60)))
ID MTL_3: Under specific conditions (number of lanes), specific minimum speeds are defined.
  • Proposition ID P4: There are two lanes in the same direction
    • Granularity: discrete
  • Proposition ID P5: There are three or more lanes in the same direction
    • Granularity: discrete
  • Proposition ID P6: Speed for left lane is at least 100 km/h in case of two lanes
    • Granularity: continuous
  • Proposition ID P7: Speed for farthest left lane is at least 110 km/h, and 90 km/h for middle lane, in case of three lanes or more
    • Granularity: continuous
  • MTL Expression: G((two_lanes=⇒(left_lane_speed ≥ 100)) ∧ (three_or_more_lanes =⇒ ((far_left_lane_speed ≥ 110) ∧ (middle_lane_speed ≥ 90))))
ID MTL_4: In case of a discrepancy between speed limit sign, drivers must follow the speed limit sign.
  • Proposition ID P8: Discrepancy exists between speed limit sign and indicated speeds
    • Ambiguity: Discrepancy is not specified, assumed as any variation between sign speed limit and indicated speeds
    • Granularity: discrete
  • Proposition ID P9: Motor vehicle is driven at the speed indicated by the speed limit sign
    • Granularity: continuous
  • MTL Expression: G((discrepancy=⇒G(vehicles_follow_sign_speed))

Dependency Sequence

From ID MTL_1 to ID MTL_2: Speed limit rules are dependent on the indication of speeds in different lanes.
From ID MTL_2 to ID MTL_3: Specific conditions based on the number of lanes are dependent on the general speed rules.
From ID MTL_3 to ID MTL_4: Discrepancy rules are dependent on the specific conditions for each lane.

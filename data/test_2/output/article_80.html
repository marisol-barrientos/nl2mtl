MTL Expressions

ID EXP1: The respective distances to maintain if a vehicle is more than or less than 100 kilometers per hour.
  • Proposition ID PROP1: The vehicle is moving at a speed greater than 100 kilometers per hour.
    • Assumption: There are functional speed indicators.
    • Assumption: No sudden speed change.
    • Granularity: Seconds
  • Proposition ID PROP2: A distance of 100 meters or more is maintained from the vehicle in front.
    • Assumption: There are functional distance indicators.
    • Assumption: Steady velocity of both vehicles.
    • Granularity: Seconds
  • Proposition ID PROP3: The vehicle is moving at a speed less than 100 kilometers per hour.
    • Assumption: There are functional speed indicators.
    • Assumption: No sudden speed change.
    • Granularity: Seconds
  • Proposition ID PROP4: The distance from the vehicle in front is less than 100m but, not less than 50 meters.
    • Assumption: There are functional distance indicators.
    • Assumption: Steady velocity of both vehicles.
    • Granularity: Seconds
  • MTL Expression: G (PROP1 =⇒ PROP2) ∧ G (PROP3 =⇒ PROP4)

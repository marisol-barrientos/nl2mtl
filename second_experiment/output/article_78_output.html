Expressway Lane Speed Limits

Regulations for speed limits in different lanes of an expressway

MTL Expressions

Expression 1: Maximum speed should not exceed 120 km/h

MTL Expression: G [0, ∞) (speed ≤ 120)

Proposition P1: Maximum speed is less than or equal to 120 km/h

Assumptions: Speed is continuously monitored

Ambiguities: No

Priority: High

Granularity: Seconds

Expression 2: Minimum speed should not be lower than 60 km/h

MTL Expression: G [0, ∞) (speed ≥ 60)

Proposition P2: Minimum speed is greater than or equal to 60 km/h

Assumptions: Constant speed monitoring

Ambiguities: No

Priority: High

Granularity: Seconds

Expression 3: Minimum speed for the left lane is 100 km/h if there are two lanes

MTL Expression: G [0, ∞) (lane_count = 2 ⇒ left_lane_speed ≥ 100)

Proposition P3: Minimum speed in left lane is 100 km/h when there are two lanes

Assumptions: Lane count is known

Ambiguities: No

Priority: Medium

Granularity: Seconds

Expression 5: Motor vehicles must follow the speed indicated by road signs in case of discrepancies

MTL Expression: G [0, ∞) (discrepancy ⇒ sign_speed)

Assumptions: Ability to detect discrepancies

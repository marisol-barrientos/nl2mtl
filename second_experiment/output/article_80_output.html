Expressway Vehicle Distance Regulation

Regulation for maintaining safe distances between vehicles on an expressway, dependent on vehicle speed.

MTL Expressions

Expression exp1: Vehicle exceeds 100 km/h must maintain a distance of at least 100 meters from the vehicle in front in the same lane.

MTL Expression: G(speed > 100 km/h ⇒ distance ≥ 100 meters)

Proposition prop1: Vehicle speed exceeds 100 km/h

Assumptions:

Ambiguities: Measurement method for speed not specified.

Priority: High

Granularity: seconds

Proposition prop2: Distance to the vehicle in front is at least 100 meters

Assumptions:

Ambiguities: Method of distance measurement not specified.

Priority: High

Granularity: meters

Expression exp2: When speed is lower than 100 km/h, distance to vehicle in front can be reduced but not less than 50 meters.

MTL Expression: G(speed < 100 km/h ⇒ distance ≥ 50 meters)

Proposition prop3: Vehicle speed is lower than 100 km/h

Assumptions:

Ambiguities: Method of speed measurement not clear.

Priority: High

Granularity: seconds

Proposition prop4: Distance to the vehicle in front is not less than 50 meters

Assumptions:

Ambiguities: Method of distance measurement not specified.

Priority: High

Granularity: meters

Non-Formalized Parts

ID: nfp1

Description: Time bounds for speed measurement and method for measuring distance are not specified.

Reason for Non-Formalization: Lack of specific details in the text.

Sequence Dependencies

Dependency: The second condition is relevant when the first is not met.
